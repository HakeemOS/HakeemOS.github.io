Typing - the right way

As someone who is constantly using a computer, becoming as profficient as possible is pretty important to me. It then became obvious to me that in order to become more efficient, it would be a good idea to try and improve my typing speed. While attempting to increase my typing speed via tools like typeracer, it occured to me that I may have made a major oversight. I wasn't following the correct, ergonomical form of typing, and my own "created form" may have upper limitations in terms of speed. I then decided to start from the ground up and learn how to type correctly.

Along this journey I have learned just how second nature typing is and how difficult it is to break habits that you are very familiar with. Even though I am improving, it is very annoying typing at a much slower pace than you are capable of, and the improvement itself is quite slowgoing. Also the foreign feeling of typing a different makes it easy to want to just type the way your are comfortable with.

Goal: 80+ words per minute.
Current: 50 words per minute.
