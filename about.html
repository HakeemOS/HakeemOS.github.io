Information about me

I am recent graduate in computer engineering at the University of Western Ontario that is working towards completing a second degree in medical physics as well. I am also the head coach of the WesternU Women's football team; a self-run contact flag football team that competes against other universities across Ontario in the OWIFA (Ontario Women's Intercollegiate Football Association) league. We completed our last season going 18-0, winning all three tournaments we participated in. In my free time I enjoy playing sports, listening to music of all genres, playing league of legends, and picking up new random skills.
